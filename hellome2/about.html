I'm Vivian Liu, and I'm a sophomore at UC Berkeley studying Cognitive Science & Computer Science & New Media.

I'm passionate about data and design. Last semester, I web-scraped and conducted research on the cultural data Youtube and Instagram can yield. My interest in data was sparked by my internship at First Data's Big Data branch. There, I prototyped a new location analysis tool that merged Census Data with their transaction data.

I devote my free time to creating. Over the past two years, I've prototyped a refresh of Reddit's UI/UX, hacked up a random biology fact generator, dabbled in bio-inspired design, etc. Check out my projects page for more.
